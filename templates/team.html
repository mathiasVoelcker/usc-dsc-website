About the Team

The goal of the DSC USC team is to create a student community of passionate and fervent developers using the latest technologies such as cloud computing, machine learning, big data, web, and app development, etc. through interactive and free workshops. We collaborate to provide students with exceptional opportunities to attend amazing events and make a meaningful impact on their communities.
